PEDIGREE® Adoption

Nearly 3.1 million dogs end up in shelters every year. Almost half never find a home.

PEDIGREE Foundation is working toward a day when all dogs are safe, secure, cared for and loved. So far, we’ve awarded more than 6,100 grants and nearly $11.6 million to shelters and rescues across the country that help dogs in need.
